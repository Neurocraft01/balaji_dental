Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your dental health and our services.

Is Root Canal Treatment (RCT) painful? expand_more
Modern RCT is performed under local anesthesia and is generally no more painful than getting a routine filling. The procedure actually aims to relieve the intense pain caused by tooth pulp inflammation or infection.
How often should I visit for a check-up? expand_more
We recommend a professional cleaning and check-up every six months for most patients. However, individuals with gum disease or those prone to cavities may need to visit more frequently.
Do you offer emergency dental services? expand_more
Yes, we prioritize dental emergencies. If you have a knocked-out tooth, severe pain, or heavy bleeding, please call our emergency hotline immediately for same-day priority care.
What insurance providers do you accept? expand_more
We accept a wide range of national and private insurance providers. Please contact our reception with your provider details so we can verify coverage for your specific treatment.
